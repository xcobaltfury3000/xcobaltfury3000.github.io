Interesting Fact

Soumith Chintala is one of the creators that have maintained one of the most important programs of Machine Learning, called PyTorch. PyTorch is one of the most widely known Machine Learning libraries that are used for applications such as computer vision as well as natural language processing. There are two core features of PyTorch, Deep Neural Networks and Tensor computing using GPUs

Education

During Soumith's stay at Vellore, India, he attended a private university by the name of Vellore Institute of Technology taking bachelor of Technology, Information Technology from 2005 to 2009. VIT University is one of the highest ranking universities in his city. Later in his life, when he travelled to America, he attended The New York University, taking Master of Science, Computer Science from 2010 to 2012.

Work and Contribution

Soumith Chintala and his co-workers created and maintained the program, Pytorch. Pytorch is one of the most important programs in Machine Learning. Not only that, but he also built various state-of-the-art machine learning system using deep learning approaches during his time as a junior research scientist at New York University. Soumith also became a research intern at a company called "Siemens" where he built systems for autonomous robot navigation involving real-time 3D registration and matching of incoming camera images. From July 2012 to August 2014, Soumith was a software engineer at Museami where he built machine learning and vision systems for mobile devices and music (IOS/Android). Lastly, for 6 years and 10 months, Soumith had multiple titles at Facebook AI including an Engineering Manager/Software Engineer, a prinicpal engineer, and a distinguished engineer. Soumith continues to help change world by creating many things in the Machine Learning field.
